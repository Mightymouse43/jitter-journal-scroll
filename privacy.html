POLICY.

Effective Date: April 17, 2025

01 / Overview

INTRODUCTION

Jitter Journal ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and website.

By using Jitter Journal, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our App.

02 / Data Collection

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via Supabase.
  • Caffeine Logs: Data you enter about beverages consumed, including drink type, caffeine amount (in milligrams), and timestamp.
  • Photos: If you grant camera permission, we access your camera solely to allow you to photograph beverages. Photos are processed locally and are not stored on our servers unless you explicitly upload them.
  • Usage Data: General interaction data such as features used and session frequency, used to improve the App experience.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for diagnostics and support.

We do not collect sensitive health data beyond caffeine intake logs you voluntarily enter.

03 / Data Use

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide core App functionality — logging, insights, and pattern tracking
  • Generate personalized AI commentary ("roasts") based on your caffeine habits
  • Send account-related communications (e.g., password resets)
  • Improve and debug the App
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

04 / Third Parties

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party service providers:

  • Supabase: Handles user authentication and secure data storage. Supabase processes your email address and authentication data. You can review Supabase's privacy practices at supabase.com/privacy.
  • OpenAI: When you photograph a beverage, the image is sent to OpenAI's API to identify the drink and estimate its caffeine content. OpenAI processes this image data on our behalf. You can review OpenAI's privacy practices at openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.
  • xAI (Grok): Your caffeine log data is sent to xAI's Grok API to generate personalized AI commentary ("roasts"). xAI processes this data on our behalf. You can review xAI's privacy practices at x.ai/privacy-policy.

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights and safety of Jitter Journal and its users.

05 / Camera Access

CAMERA & PHOTOS

Jitter Journal requests access to your device camera solely to allow you to photograph beverages for logging purposes. Camera access is optional — you can use the App fully without granting it.

We do not access your photo library without explicit permission. Photos taken within the App are not transmitted to our servers unless you choose to save them to your log. You can revoke camera permission at any time in your device Settings.

06 / Data Retention

RETENTION & DELETION

We retain your account and log data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

To request deletion of your data, contact us at automind.ai43@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."

07 / Your Rights

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at automind.ai43@gmail.com.

08 / Children

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Jitter Journal is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at automind.ai43@gmail.com.

09 / Updates

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
